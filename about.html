About Me

I'm from the Metro Detroit area and moved out to Chicago, where I currenly reside. I graduated from Eastern Michigan University in Japanese Language & Culture and I'm currently taking classes at the School of the Art Institue of Chicago for a Web Design Certificate. It's great being able to take these classes to allow me to learn and do something I truly enjoy.

About my Work

My design tends to more minimal and clean. I like to keep my designs smart, intuitive but not boring. It's also fun to research different art periods/eras and work those into digital designs. So, it's always fun to work on any projects in which I putting a specific era into the design. If you're inclined, please take a look at my resume.
