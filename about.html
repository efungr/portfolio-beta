Who am I?

This is the portfolio of Eric Fung. I'm a designer who is just having fun creating new designs out of pixels. I appreicate that you're checking out my site and hope you like some of my work. If you were Google searching my name but was looking for the Pianist or Doctor, this is not the Eric Fung you're looking for.

About Me

I'm from the Metro Detroit area, lived in Portland and Osaka at various points in my life, and now live in Chicago. I graduated from Eastern Michigan University in Japanese and I'm currently taking classes at the School of the Art Institue of Chicago for a Web Design Certificate.

About my Work

I'm a designer and a front-end developer creating responsive designs for both the big and small screens. I'm inspired by Mid-Century Modern and Bauhaus designs due to their interesting details and simplicity. This lends to my designs being clean and minimal, to focus on the content and communication.
